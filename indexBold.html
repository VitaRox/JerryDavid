Classic "Seinfeld" Episodes

What follows is a brief synopsis of some of the funniest, best, and drollest episodes of the popular, absurd, and weirdly-prescient TV comedy

1. The Gum:

In this episode, Jerry has an awkward interaction with George's "nemesis", Lloyd Braun, about a gum that he insists Jerry try.

2. The Apartment:

George attempts to "pick up" women by pretending he is married. It doesn't go well.

3. The Contest:

A frank coffeeshop discussion leads to a contest in which the winner will be crowned "Master of [their] Domain".

4. The Sniffing Accountant

Jerry's personal accountant may (or may not) be a cokehead. Hilarity ensues.

5. The Little Kicks

Elaine embarasses herself and everyone else with her terrible and weird-looking dancing at a company function.
